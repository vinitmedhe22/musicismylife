STORY BEHIND

Tanaka Guitar's journey began with our love of rock music so that we fell in love with the guitar. We started to explore the art of guitar and liked it more and more. From this we realized that music can shape the soul and society. We also see that many people like rock music and guitar instruments, so we want people to make it easy for people to buy guitar instruments. Finally Tanaka Guitar was successfully built by us.

TANAKA GUITAR

Tanaka Guitar was originally built in a small garage that only sells a few types of guitars. Unexpectedly the guitar on display in the garage always sold out every week. This makes Tanaka, which was just a small guitar shop in a garage, turned into the number 1 guitar shop in Indonesia, which has many branches in Indonesia. Tanaka Guitar provides high quality guitars from various brands and can now be purchased online.
